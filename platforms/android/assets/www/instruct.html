INSTRUCTIONS
Use the movement buttons provided at the bottom to move the player. Use the
SWIPE
button provided at the right side of the screen to align the player in the gap between the obstacles. Both Swipeup and Swipedown will align the player in the gap irrespective of its position. Tap on the rockets or crash into them to burst them. Every 20 score counts 1 level. Speed of the game increases linearly with the level.
